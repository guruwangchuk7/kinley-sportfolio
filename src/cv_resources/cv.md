emotional connection to 
place and memory. The

inspiration from the works 
of master architects such 
as Charles Correa, Peter 
Zumthor, and Louis Kahn.

02 
Pling Apartment
Year: 2026
Project Neratives
Phuentsholing is located 
in the southern part of the 
country and experiences 
a hot, humid climate. Our 
architectural response to 
these conditions was to in
corporate passive climate 
strategies such as natural 
ventilation, the stack ef
fect, and the use of materi
al mass to improve thermal 
performance. Another key 
approach was the integra
tion of greenery, including 
planters and potted plants 
along the building façade, 
to enhance the microcli
mate and soften the ar
chitectural expression.
Role & Contribution
BIM Modeller, Draftsman
